# Privacy and Licensing

## What it is

This project is open source and includes a lightweight privacy policy. The goal
is to be transparent about what data is collected and how it is used.

## Why it exists

- Learners authenticate with Google and should understand what is stored.
- Open source projects benefit from explicit licensing.

## Privacy policy

The privacy policy is published as a standalone page and linked from the UI.

Where it lives:

- Privacy policy page: [`src/app/privacy/page.tsx`](../../src/app/privacy/page.tsx)
- Header link + sign-in notice: [`src/components/site-header.tsx`](../../src/components/site-header.tsx)
